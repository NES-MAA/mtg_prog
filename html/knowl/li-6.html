Prime Numbers and Irreducible Polynomials

In the study of prime numbers and polynomials it is important to determine which polynomials are irreducible and how such polynomials are related or similar to prime numbers and prime factorization. In this presentation we would be looking at the Fundamental Theorem of Algebra, The Divisibility, Remainder Theorem,Greatest Common divisor,Euclid's Algorithm, Irreducible polynomials and some Tests for Irreducible polynomials etc. (Advisor: Jason Hardin)
