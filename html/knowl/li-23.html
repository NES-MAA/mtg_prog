The Story of Alan Turing, but not just breaking the Enigma

Alan Turing was a British mathematician and computer scientist who is very well-known for breaking the Enigma code, but had made significant amounts of contributions to mathematics, artificial intelligence, morphogenesis and computer science. We will briefly look at his major contributions, an overview of his homosexuality, his lifestyle, and his death.
