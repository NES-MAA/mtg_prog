June Stankiewicz Decker

Three Rivers Community College

June Stankiewicz Decker is Professor of Mathematics at Three Rivers Community College in Norwich Connecticut, is a member of MAA, Mathematics Association of Two Year Colleges (AMATYC) and the Mathematics Association of Two Year Colleges of Connecticut (MATYCONN). Prior to joining the Math Faculty at Three Rivers in 2002, she was a high school teacher for 10 years and part time lecturer at UCONN for 4. She holds a bachelor’s degree in Philosophy from Harvard College and a Master’s in Mathematics from UCONN. Her latest project was to co-write and then help train teachers in the Core Algebra 1 and 2 curricula for Connecticut public high schools.
