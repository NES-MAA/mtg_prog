Jason Molitierno has taught at Sacred Heart University in Fairfield, CT for the past 18 years. He has been serving as the department chair for the past eight years. Jason has served many roles in the Northeastern Section. He was vice chair of the section 2006-2007, chair of the section 2007-2009, past chair 2009-2011, Section NExT coordinator 2011-2015, and governor/section representative 2015-2018. He has also served on many section committees such as the Teaching Award Committee and various program, local arrangements, and nominating committees. On the national level, Jason served on the Committee on Sections 2011-2017 and is currently a member of the Council on Members and Communities. He recently was awarded the MAA Award for Meritorious Service.
